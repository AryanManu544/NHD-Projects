Long Before

Neerja Bhanot was born on September 7, 1963 in Chandigarh, India. She was daughter of Harish Bhanot (father) and Rama Bhanot (mother). Her father was a journalist and her mother was a housewife. Neerja had 2 brothers Akhil and Aneesh Bhanot. Neerja Bhanot received her early schooling at Sacred Heart Sen. Neerja Bhanot went to St. Xavier's College-Autonomous, Mumbai when she was old enough. Neerja studied in Bombay Scottish school when she was in her middle age. Neerja’s family used to call her Lado. Neerja Bhanot’s birth sign is Virgo. Neerja Bhanot’s brother Aneesh Bhanot was a author.

Right Before 
Neerja Bhanot’s’ major act of courage was to walk out of her marriage just within two months after she got married because she was harassed by her husband for dowry. Her mother tried to persuade Neerja to quit her job of air hostess but Neerja Bhanot loved working as a air hostess and when she was a baby, her dream was to help people in aeroplane.  Neerja Bhanot’s husband name was Naresh Mishra. After two months of Neerja’s marriage, she constantly took divorce because she was upset of the dowry. Within a year of flying with Pan Am, she was promoted from the post of cabin attendant to head purser.Neerja joined the Pan Am Flight on Jan 16,1986.
